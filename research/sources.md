Research Sources: YouTube Content Strategy for B2B SaaS

This document contains 10 YouTube creators who regularly share useful content about B2B SaaS growth, content marketing, and startup strategy. I selected these creators because they actively build, grow, or invest in SaaS businesses and share practical lessons from their own experience.

1. Simon Hoiberg

YouTube: https://www.youtube.com/@simonhoiberg

Why I chose him:
Simon is the founder of FeedHive and regularly shares videos about growing SaaS products, using AI tools, and building businesses online. His content is based on real experiments and actual business results.

Main topics:

* SaaS growth
* AI tools
* Content marketing
* Solo founder journey

2. Jonathan Rintala

YouTube: https://www.youtube.com/@JonathanRintala

Why I chose him:
Jonathan is the founder of Univid and documents how he grows a B2B SaaS company using content and video marketing. His channel focuses on practical growth strategies and real business progress.

Main topics:

* B2B SaaS growth
* Content-led growth
* Startup marketing
* Growth experiments

3. Rob Walling (MicroConf)

YouTube: https://www.youtube.com/@MicroConf
